ABOUT

Hey! My name is Francesca Longo. I am a 22 years old living in Malta. I am currently a student at the University of Malta studying for a Bachelor of Fine Arts (Honours) in Digital Arts. I enjoy creating art in numerous methods and styles, discovering new techniques and practices.

I started off with fine arts in my secondary and collage studies were I chose art as my primary subject. Here I discovered my liking for pencil work, charcoal, acrylic paint, ink, and water colours. I even started exploring methods such as collages, mixed media, and overall, experimental work. I also had the opportunity to study fine arts abroad in 2018 were I attended Cardiff Metropolitan University for a few months on an exchange.

In University, I took up Digital Arts. Here, I found my calling for illustration and graphic design. I began learning subjects including: package design, illustration (both traditional and digital), typography, photography and animation.

I have also had commissioned works for both existing and personal works. I have sold prints, hand made cards, tattoo designs, illustrations and logo designs.
